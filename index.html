#UT-Insta

#UT-Insta is a large-scale dataset consisting of more than 3 million images and their corresponding captions, collected from Instagram public profiles.

The dataset consists of two parts: One predominantly in English and the other in Persian.

Data privacy

The dataset does not contain usernames or profile pictures of the Instagram users. The images and captions are collected from public profiles only and are available to anyone on Instagram.

We also made efforts to remove username mentions, phone numbers, and email addresses from the captions.

If you have any concerns, please contact us at utinsta@ghiasi.dev.

Download dataset

English dataset is available in three variants:

  • English - 40k: Contains 40,000 images
  • English - 100k: Contains 100,000 images
  • English - Full: Contains more than 1,000,000 images

Persian dataset is available in two variants:

  • Persian - 40k: Contains 40,000 images
  • Persian - Full: Contains more than 2,000,000 images

In all of the links above, images are available in a tar file and relevant captions and hashtags are available in a gzipped json file.
